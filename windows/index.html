Our windows

Not satisfied with double gazing, ours are triple glazed. And we don’t accept typical ’developer quality’ unbranded aluminium frames: our windows are warm, eco-friendly wood.
Our bi-folds are triple glazed too. We have to use aluminium for technical reasons, so we sought out the world’s best-engineered, highest spec bi-folds.
Finally, we’re mindful you don’t want to worry about regular repainting – so our exterior frames are clad in maintenance-free aluminium.

Our triple-glazed bifold doors are manufactured by Schüco from high thermal efficiency aluminium. They’re used in high-end developments around the world – take a look at them here or check out Schüco’s brochure for their bifold doors.
Just one touch tells you all you need to know… Open a Schueco sliding door and the rigidity, effortless action and solid clunk as it closes proclaim one thing: Quality. Every Schueco bi-fold door delivers style with security, visual elegance with structural strength

In keeping with our own philosophy, our suppliers rely entirely on European manufacturing.

Our windows are made by the Danish company Rationel. They use FSC® certified wood from sustainable forests, and are certified Secured by Design.
