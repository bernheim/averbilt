We are Åverbilt

*pronounced: ove’r-bilt Homes.

Unlike some other housebuilders, we think beyond short term profit. Instead, we aim to build a reputation that will serve us over time.

So we build eco-friendly homes we’d be happy to live in ourselves, and we spend whatever it takes to meet our own very high standards.

We’re proud to build houses of exceptional quality – and we’re confident you’ll enjoy living in them.

To read more about our philosophy, just scroll down. Or, take a look at our projects and let our work speak for itself.

Our story

Åverbilt* Homes came about when Ofir Chen and Gabriel Hodes found they shared a common mindset when it came to developing and building homes: they both have a passion for each and all their projects, and an obsession with quality.

After many years of working independently, they each found it reassuring to have a partner who shared their values – and they resolved to work together to jointly pursue their ideal of building eco-friendly homes they would be proud of – and happy to live in.

Our philosophy

Most housebuilders build as cheaply as possible, while making the finished product look as luxurious as possible – to justify the price.

In other words, their focus on quality is skin deep.

More than superficial quality

Our objective is to build homes we’d be happy to live in ourselves…..so our focus on quality starts at the design stage. Our specifications call for the highest calibre materials – and energy saving features which maintain warmth at minimum running cost.

No expense is spared on our windows… (see here!)

And our family homes also include MHVR [Mechanical Ventilation & Heat Recovery] – an automatic ventilation system which filters incoming air, and where necessary extracts heat from the outgoing air for re-use.

Expensive features – but they’re what we’d want ourselves. And we believe that by strictly adhering to our own standards (and ignoring what our competitors are able to get away with) we’re building a reputation for our business which will serve us well in the future.

Our suppliers

In keeping with our own philosophy, our suppliers rely entirely on European manufacturing.

Our windows are made by the Danish company Rationel. They use FSC® certified wood from sustainable forests, and are certified Secured by Design.

We source these windows exclusively from Ecodomus, a company that shares our passion for high quality, eco-friendly and long-term house-building.
